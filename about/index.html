My Journey and Philosophy

I'm currently a Computer Science and Graphic Design student at Lehigh University, combining creativity and technology to build meaningful, interactive experiences. During my time here, I've designed mixed reality research applications featured in professional journals, participated in countless hackathons, and guided peers as an assistant teacher. I love exploring new ideas while helping others bring theirs to life.

My journey into design and development started in high school when I took elective classes in art and programming. Before that, I was building Roblox levels and making YouTube thumbnails in MS Paint, unaware that I was laying the foundation for my future. Those classes sparked a passion that led me to pursue both fields in college.

My guiding principle is knowledge. I really enjoy helping others, whether it's through teaching coding or design. I'm always curious to learn and willing to share what's possible in both design and development.
